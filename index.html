SPAM & PHISHING AWARENESS

In today's hyper-connected world, cybercriminals are increasingly using emails and messaging apps like WhatsApp to deceive people into sharing sensitive information or clicking on malicious links. It's critical to recognize these threats and safeguard our personal and professional security.

Join us in building a secure workplace by becoming a Mensa Cyber Security Champion!
✓
Do's
Verify Before You Trust: Check sender details, phone numbers, and URLs carefully.
Be Alert to Red Flags: Poor grammar, urgent demands, or requests for confidential details.
Report Suspicious Content: Immediately report to support@mensabrands.com
Use Official Channels: Always confirm through official company communication channels.
Enable Security Features: Activate 2FA on your email and WhatsApp.
✗
Don'ts
Don't Click Blindly: Avoid unexpected links or attachments in emails/WhatsApp.
Don't Share Personal Info: Never disclose passwords, OTPs, or banking details.
Don't Forward Unverified Content: Avoid spreading chain messages or fake offers.
Don't Mix Platforms: Keep work and personal communication separate.
Don't Ignore Warnings: Take device/email alerts about suspicious content seriously.
Follow these steps, don't fall for spam and phishing traps.
Stay Alert. Stay Safe. Stay Secure!
